Our company

About Pen and Chisel

Founded in March 2011 by Eileen Conner, Pen and Chisel LLC provides quality writing and editing instruction for applicants to law school programs. Our goal is to help future law students present themselves and their achievements in the most positive way possible, so as to have the best chance of admission to the law school or schools of their choice.

Pen and Chisel LLC is located in Mountain View, CA.

About Eileen

Hi! I'm Eileen Conner, and I'll be your host this evening.

After earning an MFA in creative writing from the University of Michigan, I became an active writer, editor, and teacher—but only recently have I been able to combine these. As Senior Editor for Law at Revision Editing, I helped hundreds of prospective students improve their personal statements, diversity statements, and addenda. Now, to reach even more professional school candidates, I've started Pen and Chisel LLC, providing tips, advice, and instruction to help you write great admissions essays.

Questions? Drop me a line!
