Online courses

Launch: Personal Statement

Jump-start your law school application essay

Are you starting your law school personal statement from scratch?

Are you confused about which topic to choose, what schools really want, or even where to begin? You need Launch: Personal Statement.

This course takes you through the personal statement writing process from top to bottom.

Through a series of videos and exercises, you'll build your personal statement from the ground up, finishing with an essay draft targeted to your choice of schools.

Refine: Personal Statement

Make your law school personal statement shine!

Maybe you've written and rewritten several drafts of your law school personal statement, but you aren't yet satisfied.

If you want to radically improve your essay, Refine: Personal Statement is the way to go.

Seven video classes with corresponding exercises will guide you through the most common errors that crop up in law school personal statements—and discuss how to fix them.

You'll finish strong with a highly polished and individualized final draft.
