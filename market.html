A note on the law market

Over the past few years, the law market has undergone a significant upheaval. Recent law grads are having a hard time finding good jobs and paying off their student debt. Before you decide to attend law school, we urge you to do your research and make absolutely sure that law is the right career choice for you. This is a serious problem; please take it seriously.

For those of you who have done your research and are definitely committed to becoming lawyers: we'd be delighted to help you craft top-quality admissions essays.
